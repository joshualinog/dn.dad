### **Sneak Attack** (1/turn)
- **Damage:** +1d6
- **Requires:** Finesse/ranged weapon + (Advantage OR ally within 5ft of target)

## Special Abilities

### **Cunning Action** (Level 2, Unlimited)
- **Action:** Bonus Action
- **Options:** Dash, Disengage, or Hide
- **Cannot be used with Starlight Step** (both use bonus action)

### **Starlight Step** (2/Long Rest)
- **Action:** Bonus Action
- **Effect:** Teleport 30ft to visible unoccupied space
- **Tactical Use:** Hit-and-run, positioning, escape
- **Conflict:** Cannot use with Cunning Action in same turn

### **Racial Features**
- **Keen Senses:** Perception proficiency
- **Fey Ancestry:** Advantage vs charm, immune to magical sleep
- **Astral Trance:** 4-hour long rest

## Combat Tactics Checklist

### **Standard Turn (Cunning Action)**
1. ☐ Check for Sneak Attack opportunity (advantage/ally nearby)
2. ☐ Attack with highest damage weapon
3. ☐ Use Cunning Action: Hide (bonus action) to vanish
4. ☐ Maintain stealth for advantage next turn

### **Teleport-Escape Combo**
1. ☐ Attack with Sneak Attack
2. ☐ Use Starlight Step (bonus action) to teleport 30ft away
3. ☐ Remember: Cannot use Cunning Action this turn

### **Disengage Retreat**
1. ☐ Attack with Sneak Attack
2. ☐ Use Cunning Action: Disengage (bonus action)
3. ☐ Move through danger without opportunity attacks

### **Bonus Action Decision Tree**
- **Starlight Step:** Limited (2/long rest) - save for crucial escapes
- **Cunning Action:** Unlimited - default choice for most turns
- **Cannot use both** in same turn - choose wisely!

### **Resource Management**
- **Starlight Step:** 2 uses/long rest - emergency teleports only
- **Cunning Action:** Unlimited - use every turn
- **Sneak Attack:** Once per turn - prioritize when you have advantage
- **Skills:** Stealth (+7) and Sleight of Hand (+7) are your strengths
