# Sahar - Quick Reference Sheet (Level 2)

## Core Stats (Level 2)
- AC: 14 | HP: 17 | Speed: 30ft
- STR 7(-2) | DEX 16(+3) | CON 15(+2) | INT 17(+3) | WIS 10(+0) | CHA 13(+1)
- Proficiency Bonus: +2
- Saves: Dex +5, Int +5

## Key Skills
- Stealth: +7 (Expertise)
- Sleight of Hand: +7 (Expertise)
- Investigation: +5
- Perception: +2

## Combat Actions
### Attack Options
- Dagger (Melee): +5 | 1d4+3 | Finesse, Light, Thrown
- Sacred Flame: DC 13 | 1d8 | Dex Save

### Sneak Attack (1/turn)
- Damage: +1d6
- Requires: Finesse/ranged weapon + (Advantage OR ally within 5ft of target)

## Special Abilities
- **Starlight Step:** Bonus action teleport 30ft (2 uses/long rest)
- **Cunning Action (Level 2):** Bonus action to Dash, Disengage, or Hide
  - IMPORTANT: Both Starlight Step and Cunning Action use your bonus action — you normally may not use both in the same turn.

## Level 2 Tactics Checklist
1. ☐ Check for Sneak Attack opportunity (advantage/ally nearby)
2. ☐ Attack with highest damage option
3. ☐ Use bonus action: Cunning Action (Hide/Disengage/Dash) OR Starlight Step (teleport escape/position)
4. ☐ Prepare to Hide next round if preserved

## Recommended Purchases
- Studded leather armor
- Rapier
- Extra disguise supplies, clothing, forged papers

## Roleplay Hooks
- Public harp performance to gather gossip and lull frightened children
- Moonlit Persona practice on a small, low-risk mission
- Assigned extraction of a ledger or a child to Sister Imane
