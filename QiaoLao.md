# Qiao Lao - Level 14 Protector Aasimar Theurgy Wizard

## Character Overview
- **Race**: Aasimar (Protector Variant)
- **Class**: Wizard (Theurgy Subclass) - Level 14
- **Background**: Custom (wise angelic bureaucrat)
- **Alignment**: Lawful Good (divine but dim-witted)
- **Experience Points**: 1,050,000 (Level 14)
- **Vibe**: A radiant, wing-sprouting angelic figure who wields divine magic with bureaucratic precision, despite her low intelligence.

## Ability Scores
- **Strength**: 10 (+0)
- **Dexterity**: 14 (+2) - For AC and initiative.
- **Constitution**: 14 (+2) - Moderate HP.
- **Intelligence**: 8 (-1) - Low Intel rule for hilarity (assume +1 minimum for Theurgy).
- **Wisdom**: 12 (+1)
- **Charisma**: 16 (+3) - For spellcasting and Celestial Resistance.

## Combat Stats
- **Hit Points**: 92 (14d6 + 28 from Con)
- **Armor Class**: 15 (Mage Armor)
- **Initiative**: +2
- **Speed**: 30 ft. (plus fly 30 ft. from Radiant Soul)
- **Proficiency Bonus**: +5
- **Saving Throws**: Intelligence +4, Wisdom +6
- **Skills**: Arcana +4, Religion +6, Insight +6
- **Passive Perception**: 11
- **Languages**: Common, Celestial (from Aasimar)
- **Senses**: Darkvision 60 ft.
- **Resistances/Immunities**: Celestial Resistance (half damage from radiant/necrotics); Necrotic Resistance (from Protector Aasimar).

## Features and Traits
- **Aasimar Traits (Protector)**:
  - Celestial Resistance: Half damage from radiant and necrotic.
  - Radiant Soul: Bonus action - sprout wings for 1 minute, fly 30 ft., deal +14 radiant damage once per turn on attacks/spells.
- **Theurgy Features**:
  - Divine Inspiration: Channel Divinity (e.g., Turn Undead).
  - Arcane High Priest: Access to Cleric spells; Divine Empowerment (+1 radiant damage on spells, even with low Int).
- **Wizard Features**:
  - Spell Sniper: Double range on attack spells, ignore half cover.
  - Lucky: Reroll dice once per day.
  - Tough: Extra HP (included).
- **Prepared Spells**: 23 spells (Int mod +1 for 14th level Wizard, plus domain access).

## Spells (Optimized for Duel Against Smith)
Qiao Lao prepares 23 spells. Focused on radiant/fire damage to counter Smith's flying/storm theme, control via blindness/paralysis, and spell interruption. Use Counterspell aggressively, Sunbeam for area denial, and divine buffs for survivability.

- **Cantrips (At Will)**:
  - Sacred Flame (radiant damage, Dex save; great vs. flying targets).
  - Fire Bolt (ranged fire for 14 damage).
  - Light (illuminate to counter invisibility).

- **1st Level (4 slots)**:
  - Magic Missile (auto-hit 28 damage).
  - Shield of Faith (+2 AC for 10 min).
  - Burning Hands (15 ft. cone, 21 fire damage).

- **2nd Level (3 slots)**:
  - Misty Step (teleport to evade Chain Lightning).
  - Scorching Ray (ranged 42 fire damage).

- **3rd Level (3 slots)**:
  - Counterspell (reaction to interrupt Smith's spells).
  - Fireball (20 ft. radius, 49 fire damage; Dex save DC 16 – blast flying areas).
  - Dispel Magic (remove his Greater Invisibility).

- **4th Level (3 slots)**:
  - Banishment (banish Smith for concentration, break his fly).
  - Dimension Door (teleport 500 ft., escape or reposition).

- **5th Level (2 slots)**:
  - Hold Monster (paralyze Smith; use Heightened if she had Metamagic, but focus on Wis save DC 16).

- **6th Level (1 slot)**:
  - Sunbeam (60 ft. line, 42 radiant damage per turn, blind on failed save; sustain for free to sweep the sky).

- **7th Level (1 slot)**:
  - Prismatic Wall (barrier of colors, damage/reflect spells; block Chain Lightning).

- **Cleric Spells (Theurgy Access)**:
  - 1st: Guiding Bolt (radiant damage + advantage on next attack).
  - 2nd: Spiritual Weapon (floating weapon for extra attacks).
  - 3rd: Spirit Guardians (radiant damage in aura, punish melee).
  - 4th: Guardian of Faith (summon radiant defender).
  - 5th: Flame Strike (divine fire column for 70 damage).

## Strategy for Duel
- Activate Radiant Soul immediately for flight and +14 damage. Use Sunbeam to blind and damage Smith in the air, sustaining it each turn.
- Save Counterspell for his Chain Lightning or Prismatic Spray. If he goes invisible, use Dispel Magic or just area-blast with Fireball.
- Leverage divine spells like Spirit Guardians to punish him if he lands. Use low Int for fun (e.g., on a 1, accidentally cast on herself or a "minion").
