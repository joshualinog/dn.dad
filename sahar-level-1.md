## Racial Features (Astral Elf)
- Keen Senses: proficiency in Perception
- Fey Ancestry: advantage vs. charm; immune to magical sleep
- Astral Fire: one cantrip (recommendation below)
- Starlight Step: teleport up to 30 ft as a bonus action; uses = proficiency bonus (2 per long rest)
- Astral Trance: need only 4 hours for a long rest

Recommended racial cantrip: Sacred Flame (Int-based save; 1d8 radiant on Dex save)

## Class Features (Rogue 1)
- Sneak Attack: +1d6 once per turn when you have advantage or an ally is within 5 ft of target and you use a finesse or ranged weapon
- Thieves' Cant

## Languages
- Common
- Elvish
- Thieves' Cant

## Equipment
- Armor: Leather armor
- Weapons: Rapier (recommended when available), 2 daggers (finesse, light, thrown), light crossbow
- Tools: Thieves' tools
- Pack: Burglar's pack (or equipment from selection)
- Starting gold: 4d4 × 10 gp (average 100 gp)

Recommended equipment priorities:
1. Studded leather armor (level 2+)
2. Rapier or higher-precision finesse weapon
3. Items that boost mobility/stealth

## Attack Options
- Dagger (melee): +5 to hit, 1d4+3 piercing (finesse, light, thrown 20/60)
  - With sneak attack: 1d4 + 3 + 1d6
- Dagger (thrown): +5 to hit, 1d4+3 (range 20/60)
- Sacred Flame (racial cantrip, if chosen): Save Dex (DC 13), 1d8 radiant (no attack roll)

## Tactics & Role
- Role: Mobile skirmisher / precision striker. Hit-and-run specialist leveraging Starlight Step and, later, Cunning Action.
- Primary tactic: Use Stealth to gain advantage, apply Sneak Attack, then use Starlight Step to escape/position.
- Personality: Acrobatic, lunar-themed, precise, takes pride in mobility and technique.

## Leveling Notes
- Level 2: Cunning Action (Dash, Disengage, Hide as bonus action)
- Level 3: Arcane Trickster — gain spellcasting (focus on Illusion/Enchantment), Mage Hand Legerdemain, 2 cantrips & 3 1st-level spells
- Level 4: ASI / Feat (consider Dex to 18 or Mobile feat)
